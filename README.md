# 📊 Business Sales Performance Analytics

## 🔍 About the Project
This project analyzes business sales data to identify revenue trends, 
top-selling products, high-value categories, and regional performance. 
Completed as part of FutureInterns Data Science & Analytics Internship - Task 1.

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies Used
- Python
- Pandas
- NumPy
- Matplotlib
- Jupyter Notebook

## 📁 Dataset
- Source: Superstore Sales Dataset (Kaggle)
- Records: 9,994 rows and 21 columns

## 🔎 Analysis Performed
- KPI Summary (Total Sales, Profit, Orders, Customers)
- Regional Sales & Profit Performance
- Category wise Sales Analysis
- Top 10 Products by Sales
- Yearly Sales Trend Analysis

## 💡 Key Insights
- Total Revenue of 2.29 Million USD with ~12.5% profit margin
- West region is the top performer with 14.9% profit margin
- Central region shows red flag with only 7.9% profit margin
- Furniture category critically underperforming with only 2.4% profit margin
- Canon imageCLASS 2200 is the star product — nearly double the second best!
- Business shows consistent 20% YoY growth in 2017
